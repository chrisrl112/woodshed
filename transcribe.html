1 · Select a tune

Pick a prioritized Real Book tune already in the Woodshed. We render its page and slice it into lines for you.

2 · Parse the page into lines

Cuts the page into per-system strips (no LLM). Then eyeball them — uncheck any line that isn't melody (pickup-only staves, bass-clef intros) so it's skipped.

3 · Transcribe

Runs the homr reader on each kept line, on your Mac, and assembles a draft. This can take a bit — homr reads one staff at a time.

4 · Review & correct

Each line on its own staff. Click a notehead to select, then nudge. Anchor drops a whole line onto its correct first note (fixes homr's octave/clef slips). Edits flow straight to the ABC below.
